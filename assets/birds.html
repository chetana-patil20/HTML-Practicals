Green Parrot A green parrot is a bright green bird with a curved beak, sharp claws, and expressive eyes. It is known for its playful nature, intelligence, and ability to mimic human sounds. Green parrots often have long tails and smooth feathers, making them look vibrant and lively. They usually travel in groups and enjoy eating fruits, seeds, and grains..
Sparrow A sparrow is a small, brownish-grey bird with a short tail and a tiny pointed beak. Sparrows are very active, quick, and commonly found around houses, gardens, and streets. Their chirping is soft and pleasant. They mostly eat grains, seeds, and tiny insects. Sparrows are friendly and used to living close to people.
Pigeon A pigeon is a medium-sized bird with a plump body, smooth feathers, and a small head. Pigeons are usually grey with shiny green and purple shades on their neck. They are calm, gentle birds that fly smoothly and are often seen in cities. Pigeons feed on grains, seeds, and crumbs.
